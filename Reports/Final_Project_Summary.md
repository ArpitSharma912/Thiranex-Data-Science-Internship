Titanic Data Cleaning and Visualization

To clean the Titanic Dataset, handle missing values, visualize important partners, and detect outliers.

- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn

- Data Visualization

The project successfully cleaned and analyzed the Titanic dataset. The visualization provided insights into passenger demographics and survival patterns, making the dataset suitable for future machine learning tasks.
